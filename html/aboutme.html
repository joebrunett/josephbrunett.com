About Me

Hello my name is Joe Brunett and I am a film/television major at the Savannah College of Art and Design with a minor in Sound Design. I have worked on a variety of films doing many different positions. These positions include Grip, Key Grip, Best Boy, Gaffer, Director, 2nd Assistant Director, 1st Assistand Director, Director, Producer, Assistant Editor, and Editor.

My focus is editing. I can use programs such as Avid Media Composer, Adobe Premiere Pro and Final Cut Pro. I have edited many different kinds of films including Music Videos, Narratives and shorts.

Feel free to reach out to me for anything via my contact page or any social media. Cheers!
